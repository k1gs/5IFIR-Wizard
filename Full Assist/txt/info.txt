Full Assist mode allows you to set-up the main 4IFIR parameters to ensure that your console gets the full overclocking experience.

For every parameter - there is a help available by pressing the Y button. Make sure to read it to understand the impact of your actions.

After selecting a value the console will reboot. 

Return to the 4IFIR Wizard after the reboot to continue the process.
