This parameter controls the RAM frequency of the 4IFIR Maximized option.

Different modes exist for different scenarious: 

ECO - Light handheld gaming
Default - Handheld gaming in 60 FPS
SPORT - Heavy games or gaming in docked mode

To test this setting - select 4IFIR Maximized in 4IFIR RedRat and play some games.

If you see artifacts or glitches - come back and lower the setting.
